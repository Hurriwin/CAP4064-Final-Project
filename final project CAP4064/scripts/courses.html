Brief Description of Courses

The following are common exercises that we encourage our client to do as part of their daily exercise routine.

French
French 1 is an introductory course to the French language, designed for beginners who have little to no prior knowledge of French. In this course, I learned the basics of the French language, including grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, and cultural aspects of the French-speaking world.
Advanced C#
In advanced C# class it is typically designed for programmers who already have a strong foundation in C# programming and want to further develop their skills. In this course, I learned advanced concepts and techniques for C# programming, as well as best practices for software development.
Health Modern Living
In this general health class it is designed to provide students with an overview of various aspects of health and wellness. In this class, I learned about different factors that affect health and how to maintain a healthy lifestyle such as exercising, healthy eating choices, the affect of drugs and many other topics.
